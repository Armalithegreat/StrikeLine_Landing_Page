How it works

Three steps.
That's all.

01
Pick your strike

Choose any asset and set your own strike price. The price you believe it will hit — or miss — before expiry. Your terms, not ours.

02
Call it up or down

Predict whether the price lands above or below your strike at expiry. Optionally apply up to 2× leverage to amplify your position.

03
Settle on-chain

At expiry, the oracle resolves your position on Sui. No intermediaries. No custodians. Trustless and transparent every time.
